# Chime!
### Sell and order food in Miagao with just one app

Chime! is a mobile app customized for food enthusiasts in Miagao, Iloilo.

The app has supports:
- Menu of the Day
- Store announcements
- Product categories
- Product variants
- Limited stock products
- Delivery Landmarks
and much more!
